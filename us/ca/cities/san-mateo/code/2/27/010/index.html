2.27.010 ORGANIZATION.

A park and recreation commission shall continue as heretofore established in and for the City, to be known and designated as the "San Mateo park and recreation commission." It shall consist of five members appointed by the Council.

Each member of the commission shall serve at the pleasure of the Council for a term of four years and until the appointment and qualification of a successor.


(Ord. No. 1976-4 § 1; prior code § 10.07.)
