23.28.150 RECORDS REPORTING—ADDED.

Section 901.6.2 "Records" is amended to include subsection 901.6.2.2 "Records Reporting" to read as shown below:

Fire detection, alarm and extinguishing systems, shall be maintained in an operative condition at all times, and shall be replaced or repaired where defective. Non-required fire protection systems and equipment shall be inspected, tested, and maintained or removed. All inspection, testing and maintenance reports shall be forwarded to the Fire Department using electronic media. No paper (hard copy) reports shall be permitted.


(Ord. No. 2016-12 § 3.)
