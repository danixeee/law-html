23.24.040 INFEASIBILITY EXEMPTION.

If an applicant for a Covered Project believes that circumstances exist that makes it infeasible to meet the requirements of this Chapter, the applicant may request an exemption as set forth in Section 23.06.015 of the Municipal Code. In applying for the exemption, the burden is on the Applicant to show infeasibility.


(Ord. No. 2016-5 § 1.)
