27.64.270 OTHER USES.

For uses not listed heretofore in the schedules of parking requirements, parking spaces shall be provided on the same basis as required for the most similar listed use, or as determined by the Zoning Administrator.


(Ord. No. 1986-13 § 1; Ord. No. 1978-18 § 101; prior code § 151.05(K).)
