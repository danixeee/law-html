7.41.060 ENFORCEMENT OF APPLICABLE LAW.

If an agent or employee of the tobacco retailer violates any provisions of this chapter or any federal or state tobacco-related law, the tobacco retailer shall immediately report the violation to the Police Department and the Environmental Health Division.


(Ord. No. 2015-16 § 1; Ord. No. 2008-12 § 1.)
