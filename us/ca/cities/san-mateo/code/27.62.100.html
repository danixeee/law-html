27.62.100 LANDSCAPING.

Landscaping for setbacks, parking areas, open space and any other area of the development shall be required in accordance with applicable standards and policies promulgated heretofore or hereafter by resolution of the City Council.


(Ord. No. 1971-41 § 1; prior code § 152.09(II)(H).)
