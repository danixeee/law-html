11.28.036 TRUCK ROUTES—SCHEDULE.

The following are designated as streets the use of which is permitted to any vehicle exceeding the maximum gross weight of five (5) tons (GVWR):

o US 101 (Bayshore Freeway)

o State Route 82 (El Camino Real)

o State Route 92 (J. Arthur Younger Freeway)

o 2nd Avenue between South Humboldt Street and North Amphlett Boulevard

o East 3rd Avenue (J. Hart Clinton) from the easterly city limits to US 101 (between the hours of eight-thirty a.m. to four-thirty p.m. eastbound)

o East 3rd Avenue between US 101 and South Claremont Street

o East 4th Avenue between US 101 and South Claremont Street

o 9th Avenue between El Camino Real and South Claremont Street

o 19th Avenue between South Delaware Street and Fashion Island Boulevard

o East 25th Avenue between El Camino Real and South Delaware Street

o 28th Avenue between Edison Street and El Camino Real

o 37th Avenue between El Camino Real and Edison Street

o Bridgepointe Parkway between Baker Way and Bridgepointe Circle

o South Claremont Street between 3rd Avenue and 9th Avenue

o Concar Drive between Westbound SR 92 Ramps and Grant Street

o South Delaware Street between Garvey Way and 25th Avenue

o Edison Street between 28th Avenue and the Hillsdale Shopping Center

o Fashion Island Boulevard between 19th Avenue and Baker Way

o Franklin Parkway between US 101 and Saratoga Drive

o East Hillsdale Boulevard between El Camino Real and US 101

o East Hillsdale Boulevard between US 101 and easterly city limit

o South Humboldt Street between 2nd Avenue and 4th Avenue

o Kehoe Avenue between Northbound US 101 Ramps and South Norfolk Street (between the hours of six a.m. to six p.m. Monday through Friday)

o Mariner's Island Boulevard between Eastbound SR 92 Ramps and Fashion Island Boulevard

o South Norfolk Street between East 3rd Avenue and Fashion Island Boulevard (between the hours of six a.m. to six p.m. Monday through Friday)

o The southerly side of Peninsula Avenue, between Bayshore Freeway and El Camino Real

o East Poplar Avenue between Southbound US 101 Ramps and Amphlett Boulevard Street

o Saratoga Drive between Franklin Parkway and East Hillsdale Boulevard


(Ord. No. 2011-4 § 1; Ord. No. 2008-10 § 2.)
