5.24.200 SCHOOL, PRIVATE.

Each person conducting the business of private school shall pay an annual business license tax of $50.00.


(Ord. No. 2004-10 § 1; Ord. No. 1984-5 § 53; Ord. No. 1977-20 § 25.)
