26.16.010 SEWAGE DISPOSAL.

(a) Disposal of all sewage shall be by means of public sanitary sewers. Sewers and laterals shall be installed and connected to existing systems in accordance with the design standards and specifications on file in the public works department. Sizing of facilities, which may also serve areas beyond the subdivision, or which may be expected to serve nonresidential uses, shall be as required by the City engineer.

(b) No proposal for any subdivision shall result in violation of existing requirements prescribed by the San Francisco Bay Regional Water Quality Control Board pursuant to the California Water Code.


(Ord. No. 1985-24 § 10; Ord. No. 1984-20 § 1; Ord. No. 1982-32 § 1; Ord. No. 1969-8 § 1; prior code § 139.15 (c).)
