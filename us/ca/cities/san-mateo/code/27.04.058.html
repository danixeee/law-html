27.04.058 BICYCLE PARKING FACILITY.

"Bicycle parking facility" means a space exclusively for the storage of bicycles.


(Ord. No. 2012-5 § 1; Ord. No. 2011-10 § 2; Ord. No. 1979-7 § 1.)
