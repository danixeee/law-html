8.02.290 SCHEDULE OF FEES AND CHARGES (County § 6.04.290).

Animal Control Program fees and charges established by this Code shall be as adopted and from time to time amended by the County of San Mateo and set forth in Section ‍6.04.290 of the San Mateo County Code.


(Ord. No. 2005-15 § 18; Ord. No. 2005-9 § 1; Ord. No. 1995-11 § 2; Ord. 1990-25, §§ 2 & 3, 1990; Ord. 1990-8 §§ 8 & 9, 1990; Ord. No. 1988-13 § 1; Ord. No. 1987-24 § 5; Ord. No. 1985-27 § 2.)
