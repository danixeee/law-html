13.30.030 SCOPE OF DEDICATIONS.

Only lands owned by the City of San Mateo which are over 4 acres in area may be dedicated by the City Council in its discretion; provided, however, that lands of less than 4 acres may be dedicated when they are contiguous with and incorporated into lands previously dedicated. Easements of record shall not be eliminated by a dedication.


(Ord. No. 2007-1 § 3; Ord. No. 1989-13 § 1.)
