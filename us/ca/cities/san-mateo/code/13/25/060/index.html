13.25.060 FEES.

Upon payment of user fees, the City of San Mateo shall provide a sticker to be visibly affixed to the starboard side of the windshield of boats so equipped, or positioned near the stern on the starboard side of the hull. Single daily use fees in and out shall be evidenced by a receipt for payment of said fee. Boats propelled solely by hand or sail shall be exempt from Marina Lagoon use fees unless using the boat ramp.


(Ord. No. 2007-1 § 3.)
