13.28.020 VIOLATIONS—A MISDEMEANOR.

Any person violating any of the provisions or failing to comply with any of the mandatory requirements, of Sections 3.68.010 through 3.68.160, as mentioned in Section 13.28.010, is guilty of a misdemeanor or infraction as charged pursuant to Chapter 1.04.


(Ord. No. 2007-1 § 3; Ord. No. 1990-8 § 3; Ord. No. 1981-11 § 1.)
