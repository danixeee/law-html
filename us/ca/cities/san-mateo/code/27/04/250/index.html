27.04.250 HOTEL, APARTMENT.

"Apartment hotel" means a building containing dwelling units or individual guest rooms, the majority of which are for permanent guests. Maid and janitor service may be provided but kitchen facilities are not necessarily included.


(Ord. No. 1978-18 § 32; prior code § 142.01(84).)
