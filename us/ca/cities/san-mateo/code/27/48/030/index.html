27.48.030 SPECIAL USES.

Special Uses in the E1 district may also be allowed subject to approval of a special use permit. Residential units on parcels without a residential overlay district classification subject to R4 district "Minimum Development Standards" in Section 27.24.040 and affordable housing requirements as adopted by City Council resolution, unless otherwise specified in Chapter 27.29; however, secondary units are prohibited.


(Ord. No. 2010-15 § 13; Ord. No. 1992-2 § 33; Ord. No. 1991-18 § 25; Ord. No. 1985-17 § 44; Ord. No. 1981-27 § 38; Ord. No. 1976-5 § 9; Ord. No. 1971-4 § 2; Ord. No. 1970-14 § 3; Ord. No. 1969-34 § 2; prior code § 148.5.03(B).)
