27.61.120 APPLICATION PROCEDURES.

Except for reclassification initiated by the Planning Commission or City Council, all applications for SC classification shall be accompanied by a complete application for a senior citizen special use permit. Submittal requirements for senior citizen special use permits shall be identical to those provided for in this title for other special use permits, except for those additional matters required by the senior citizen regulations.


(Ord. No. 1978-11 § 1.)
