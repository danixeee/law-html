27.02.170 CONSISTENCY WITH GENERAL PLAN.

In their review of planning applications, each approval body involved shall consider whether the planning application is in conformance with the San Mateo City General Plan. This shall specifically include those areas where the general plan does not permit high-rise structures, regardless of the underlying zoning.


(Ord. No. 1981-14 § 2; Ord. No. 1978-18 § 1.)
