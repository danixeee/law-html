27.17.040 SPAR.

A manufactured home unit on a permanent foundation on a private lot may be permitted subject to the approval of a site plan and architectural review (SPAR) application by the Zoning Administrator. The applicant/owner shall also provide the information required on the MH Form. Subsequent modifications, such as, room additions, shall be subject to issuance of a building permit.


(Ord. No. 1992-15 § 11; Ord. No. 1985-17 § 19; Ord. No. 1981-22 § 1.)
