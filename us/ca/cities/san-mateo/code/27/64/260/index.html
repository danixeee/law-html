27.64.260 MIXED OR MULTIPLE USES.

When two (2) or more uses are located on the same zoning plot or within the same building, parking spaces equal in number to the sum of the separate requirements for each such use shall be provided. No parking space or portion thereof shall serve as a required space for more than one (1) use unless otherwise authorized by the Planning Commission in accordance with Title 27. Chapters 27.06 through 27.12, 27.62, 27.74, 27.78 and 27.80.


(Ord. No. 1986-13 § 1; Ord. No. 1978-18 § 101; prior code § 151.05(J).)
