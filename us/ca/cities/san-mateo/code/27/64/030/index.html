27.64.030 FACILITIES—EXISTING.

Accessory off-street parking or loading facilities which are located on the same parcel as the building or use served and which were in existence on June 18, 1986 or were provided voluntarily after such date, shall not hereafter be reduced below, or if already less than, shall not further be reduced below the requirements of this title for a similar new building or use.


(Ord. No. 1986-13 § 1; prior code § 151.02(B).)
