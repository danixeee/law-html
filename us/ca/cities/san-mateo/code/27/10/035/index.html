27.10.035 APPLICATIONS FOR DEMOLITION PERMITS.

Demolition permits shall not be issued unless the requirements set forth in Section 23.06.035 are met.


(Ord. No. 1989-18 § 7; Ord. No. 1985-17 § 19.)
