8.24.020 PERMIT—REVOCATION.

No person shall maintain a stable for any horse, mule or cow, or any number thereof, without first having obtained from the board of zoning adjustments a permit therefor. Such special permit shall be subject to revocation by the board of zoning adjustments upon any failure on the part of such person to comply fully with any of the requirements of the special permit.


(Ord. No. 1982-1 § 7; prior code § 109.03.)
